About Us

Going Green is staying CLEAN

AZoom Electric Car Rental

AZoom Car Rental specialises in renting electric automobiles to everyone. For our ecologically concerned consumers, we provide an appealing selection of electric vehicles to meet a variety of demands and situations.

By driving an EV, you are helping to minimise hazardous air pollution caused by exhaust emissions. An electric automobile emits no greenhouse gases since it is powered by a battery rather than burning fuel inside. It also necessitates fewer of the more hazardous, polluting materials, such as engine oil and coolant fluids.
